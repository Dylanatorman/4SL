Datasets for 4StudentLives website charts

1) edweek_school_shootings_2018_2024.csv
   - Metric: Count of K-12 school shootings WITH injuries or deaths, by calendar year.
   - Source: Education Week tracker and "School Shootings This Year: How Many and Where" hub.
   - URL (2025 hub): https://www.edweek.org/leadership/school-shootings-this-year-how-many-and-where/2025/01
   - Values extracted from EdWeek summary text:
     2018: 24
     2019: 24
     2020: 10
     2021: 35
     2022: 51
     2023: 38
     2024: 39

2) cdc_youth_suicide_rate_10_24_2011_2021.csv
   - Metric: Suicide death rate per 100,000 population for ages 10-24, national, by calendar year.
   - Source: CDC National Center for Health Statistics, National Vital Statistics System (NVSS).
   - Data Brief 471 tables (Figure 1 data table): https://www.cdc.gov/nchs/data/databriefs/db471-tables.pdf
   - Rates used: 2011–2021 inclusive (10–24 years).

Notes:
- Definitions differ across sources. EdWeek includes only incidents with at least one injury or death on K-12 property.
- CDC rates are age-specific, national, and age-adjusted per NVSS conventions for 10–24.
- For consistency, do not mix these series with other definitions without labeling.
